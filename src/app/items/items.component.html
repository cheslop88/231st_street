231st Street Jazz Club

Come and experience an authentic 1920s speakeasy hidden away in Midtown Manhattan

Built around 1915, this speakeasy and later a jazz club was the once favoured haunt of many of New York's mobsters in the 1920s.

We have maintained many of the original period features of the time, including the speakeasy passageways and exits.
